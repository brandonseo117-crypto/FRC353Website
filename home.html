Who we are

Who are we? Who are we?

We are FIRST Team 353! Every year we participate in the FIRST Robotics Competition, where we are given six weeks to design, prototype, build, and test a robot to compete at events with teams from across the world. Along the way, we impact our school and community with many different events to spread STEM everywhere.

Sponsors

Our club is supported through generous sponsors who provide funding to help us build amazing robots and grow our program each year.

Leadership

Our team is guided by student leaders from grades 10 through 12, with work divided across business and build teams and their subgroups.

Get involved

We are always looking for mentors, students, and other FIRST teams who want to learn, collaborate, and make an impact.
